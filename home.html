About Us

HISTORY

Mahallah Asma’ was established since 1996.
Mahallah Asma’ Office consist of 4 staff i.e. one (1) Principal and few fellows,
one (1) Assistant Hostel Manager, and two (2) Administrative Assistants.
Since then, we at Mahallah Asma’ strives hard to provide the best quality services for the residents.
Mahallah Asma’ has 7 blocks which can accommodate approximately 856 students i.e. 40 UG and PG students.
Apart accommodation, we also provide other range of services including
café, convenient shop, laundry, wireless internet, and sports equipments.
